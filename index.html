Cook like an Egyptian

I am writing a blog exploring the history of food, from ancient times to the modern cuisine, delivered in a cooking book format.Food is one of the most essential aspects of human life, but how did we get from hunting and gathering to cooking and farming? In this intro to my food blog, we will explore the history of food production, from the stone age to the modern cuisine. We will see how humans adapted to different environments, climates, and cultures, and how they developed new techniques, technologies, and tastes. We will also examine the challenges and opportunities that food production faces in the present and the future.
