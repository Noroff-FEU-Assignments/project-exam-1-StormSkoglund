Who am I?

Hi, I’m Alex Skoglund.

I’m an archeologist who loves food and has a keen interest in the history of food.

I have traveled around the world to explore different cuisines and cultures, and I have read through historical sources to come up with long lost recipes. I believe that food is not only a way of nourishing the body, but also a way of connecting with the past and understanding the people who lived before us.

On my blog, I share my discoveries and experiments with ancient and medieval dishes, such as Roman garum, Viking skyr, and Aztec chocolate.

I also give tips on how to recreate these dishes at home using modern ingredients and techniques. My blog is a treasure trove for anyone who wants to learn more about the origins and evolution of food, and to taste some of the flavors that have been forgotten by time.
